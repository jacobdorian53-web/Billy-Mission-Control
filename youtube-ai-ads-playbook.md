## ðŸ“± Funnel #2: AI Shorts â†’ Quiz Funnel

**Result:** Single ad drove $1M+ in spend. Brand does $10M/month on YouTube.

### Setup
- **Format:** YouTube Shorts
- **Volume:** 400+ creatives/month (mostly AI-generated)
- **Funnel:** AI Ad â†’ Quiz Page â†’ Offer

### Winning Shorts Structure (Tai Chi Example)

| Element | Execution |
|---------|-----------|
| **Hook** | Hard qualifier ("ladies over 220 lbs") + specific method + deadline urgency + start urgency |
| **Enemy** | Clear villain (the gym). "The gym breaks you. This heals you." |
| **Proof** | Relatable proxy ("your mother"), age validation, bold transformation promise with timeline |
| **Objection Handling** | Turn weaknesses into strengths. "Haven't worked out in years? That's even better." |
| **Friction Removal** | No equipment, 9 minutes/day, body is enough |
| **Soft CTA** | 3 simple steps: click, get app, start. No price/commitment mention. |

### Why It Works
- **Specificity sells:** "Ladies over 220 lbs" not "overweight women"
- **Concrete timelines:** "By Christmas" "End of November" "9 minutes"
- **Every line removes an obstacle**
- **Dialogue format disguises aggressive objection handling**
